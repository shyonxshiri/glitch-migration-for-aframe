Shyons Portfolio


These works were created in 2020 in preparation to apply for the DMA BFA program.

Below is a grid of various pages that each consist of one of my own works.

This first image was the first attempt i had made in my Photoshopping skills. I had mashed together images of both my friends car and mine as well as the city of San Francisco in the background. My goal with this assignment was to create a image of how different life could be at the moment if Covid-19 had not occured.

This the second project i had worked on using Photoshop. Here i had mized a picture of Dubai witha photo i had taken at the beach. I blended the ocean with the dim clouds that surround the city just for the sake of a nice visual. I also used effects for the image to distort the city and make the lights and color seem electric.

This is one of the memes that i had made for my Digital Media Art class. The focus of these few projects were to poke fun at some of the instigations that were put in place due to Covid, and how some people have handled these laws.

This is another template of the Covid memes that i have made. This meme had meant a little more to me because i enjoy a good drift.
